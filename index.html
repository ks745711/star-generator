Star Generator

This is a javascript-based generative web application. Use the controls on the right to manipulate the variables.

Parameters

  • Show introduction: Show or hide this text
  • Points: The amount of points on the star
  • Inner radius: The inside radius of the star
  • Outer radius: The outside radius of the star
  • Star outline: The border on the star
  • Text: Custom text on top of the star
  • Text size:The size of custom text
  • Text color: The color of the custom text
  • Star color: The color of the star
  • BG color: The color of the background
